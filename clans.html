DoomLegends Clans

DoomLegends clan information - useful if you are confused or just interested in the system!

Creating/Managing a Clan

You can create a Clan in the /clans menu for $25,000 by clicking the emerald icon.
You can access your clan by clicking on the Golden Helmet icon in /clans.
You can manage members in your Clan menu by clicking on the player heads in the third row of the chest. In there you can invite members, unlock new slots, or manage existing members.

Clan Tokens and Other Stats

Clan Level is the Level of your clan, which can be upgraded after passing multiple requirements, such as Total Clan Points, Reputation, and more!
Clan Tokens are the main currency used in Clans. Each kill a Clan member gets gives 1 Clan Token towards the Clan. These can be spent in many shops, including the Upgrade Shop, Boosters, Clan Icons and more!
Reputation is a measure of kills-to-deaths in your Clan, and is a good way of gauging the skill level of your clan. This is not the same as a kill-to-death-ratio, and is instead the total number of (kills - deaths). This stat is capped at -500 and 500 either side.
Rating: see 'Clan Rating' section below

Clan Level

Not much has been developed for this mechanic, however each Level unlocks the capability for new things, such as Clan Icons, Banner Patterns, Upgrades, Boosters and more!

Clan Rating

Clan Rating is a general basis on how good your Clan is, by showing it as a comprehendable number, while also allowing you to compare your Clan against others. Rating is a combination of:
• Clan Tokens (cap: 4)
• Average Clan Member KD (this is scaled up a bit, and does show your actual avg KD; cap: 5)
• Reputation (cap: 4)
• Clan Level (cap: 2)

Clan Rating: Calculations

Clan Tokens Rating = 0.055(tokens^0.55)
Average Clan Members KD Rating = total kd / members * 1.667
Reputation Rating = 0.11225(reputation^0.575)
Level Rating = level / 7.5

Clans Competitive

Clans Competitive adds a competitive nature to the Clans system, by allowing you to go against other Clans and recieve rewards for doing so.

Not much has been developed for this mechanic so far, and many things are unfinished here.

Clan Icons

Each Clan starts off with an Oak Sign as their Clan Icon, however, each Clan can purchase a numerous amount of Minecraft Blocks that they can use as their Clan Icon, allowing them to vary in the Clans Menu.

Clan Icons: Banner Patterns

Once passing Clan Level V (5), a Clan can use Banner patterns as their Clan Icon. They can buy Banner Patterns, Dyes and Banner Bases from a Banner Shop for Clan Points.
